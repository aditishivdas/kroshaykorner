— Our Story

A small handmade corner.

KroshayKorner is a small handmade corner where four creators bring imagination into reality through crochet. Every knot, every pattern, and every design is made with dedication and love.

We started this little brand out of our hostel rooms at IIT Patna — a shared love for soft yarn, slow afternoons, and making people smile.
